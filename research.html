Primordial black holes

Primordial black holes (PBHs) are black holes that might have formed very soon after the Big Bang, and which could potentially explain the dark matter in our universe. As a member of the MIT PBH group, I study how PBHs would evolve under the conditions of the early universe.

Undergraduate research

As an undergraduate, I did two research internships in astrophysics - one at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics and one at the Nicolaus Copernicus Astronomical Center - and my undergraduate thesis project was in lattice QCD.
